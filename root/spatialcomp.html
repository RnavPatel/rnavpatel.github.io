Creating an adventure in a space.

Imagine if the same room could transport you to multiple worlds. Project Wander aims to achieve this mission. Through the help of “slug-guides,” people can make maps of worlds and place them within the same space, creating digital destinations and breadcrumbs for “adventurers” in the same room to experience.
